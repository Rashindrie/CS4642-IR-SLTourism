Spaghetti & Co is a Hikkaduwa institution, serving up Italian fare for as long as I can remember. They have a really big verandah and the food is pretty good, if not amazing.

The Food

The items here are mainly Italian, mostly pizzas and pastas. We tried one of both. First the bacon and mushroom pizza (Rs. 950 for a 10"). This is a hefty portion even for two and not a bad value. The pizza is nice (can't go wrong with bacon) but not amazing. It is one of the better pizzas in Hikkaduwa.

They give you spices to add to your pizza as well, which is nice.

We also tried the prawn and pesto gnocchi (Rs. 990). Again, a hefty portion but nothing magical flavorwise. The gnocchi was heavy and the pesto and prawn wasn't a great combination. This pasta was all right, but nothing to write home about.

Our overall impression of the food here was that it's average Sri Lankan Italian.

The Location/Ambience

Spaghetti & Co has a big spacious verandah at the end of the Hikkaduwa Strip (on Galle Road). It's really big and airy and well lit. It's a great location, set back in a garden home.

Service

When we went it wasn't busy at all. The service was quite good, just a nice lady who took our order and brought it out.

Conclusion

Spaghetti & Co isn't the best Italian food you'll get on the coast, but it's pretty good and has a really nice location.


TIP

During the height of the tourist season, December- Jan, they are absolutely packed every evening so book ahead or arrive early.
