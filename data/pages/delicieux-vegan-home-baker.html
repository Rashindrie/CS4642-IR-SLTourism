With a new year comes a new fitness and health resolution: eating less, cutting down on excess sugary items or turning vegetarian/vegan. Also, many people equate a vegan diet with bland recipes prepared without eggs, butter, meat or other animal products that are certain to be tasteless and boring.

Enter Délicieux by Manesha Peiris. I was recently invited to a bake sale at Amro Vegetarian Café by Manesha and was honestly skeptical about the taste of her baked goods after she mentioned she prepared all her items using key ingredients such as coconut milk and vegetable oil.

The Klubnika


However, I was pleasantly surprised by the silky smooth flavour of the Klubnika; a dark chocolate cake with a strawberry puree filling with a double layer chocolate coating, topped with fresh strawberries and dark chocolate sprinkles. I licked my spoon clean!



Besides chocolate cake, there were fudge brownies with almonds, mocha brownies with berries, chocolate rosette cake, death by coffee shots in marmalade & mochachino and black velvet cupcakes. All vegan, all delicious.

For the adventurous sweet tooth, Manesha also offers vodka, beer and rum infused cakes, cupcakes and brownies with a kick. For a vegan or sinful chocolatey indulgence, we recommend the Dark Chocolate Klubnika. Order now, thank us later.
