D'Decor sells fabric, sofas, mirrors and other household accesories. It's located on De Fonseka Road, directly opposite Qbaa. 

They have a range of classy high-end furniture on display, and friendly staff to guide you through the place.

Their main focus is fabric, of which they have a massive selection. They have several areas designated for a variety of types and colours of fabric which can be used as curtains and also cushion covers to personalize your home. The price range of these vary according to the type and quantity of the materials purchased.

They get all their furniture and designs down from the USA and they occasionally import furniture for their clients as well. Apart from the selection in their showroom, they also have an online catalogue for sofas and lamps/accessories that you can browse through and order from.

They offer a variety of household accessories like ornaments and lamps, which you can use to bling up your home. I particularly loved the mirrors there because of its seriously dope and unique frames.

If you aren't sure how any of this will fit in your home or if you don't know how to mix and match the sofas with cushions, fear not — D'Decor has an in-house interior designer that will see to your every need. 

Conclusion

D'Decor has a nice selection to choose from. So, if you're planning on buying some new furniture — I recommend you start here.
