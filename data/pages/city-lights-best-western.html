City Lights is now VU Restobar.

Best Western Elyon is one of Colombo's newest hotels and City Lights is their rooftop bar. It has a nice view over Colombo 5 and a great ambience. Bit expensive and the service is more geared for hotel guests, but it's a nice spot if you're in the neighborhood.

Location

The Best Western Elyon is at the Kirulapone end of Baseline Road. It's actually not in central Colombo, but it is in Colombo 5 and close to Nugegoda and Kohuwala. The main draw of City Lights is the view which, while not quite a cityscape, is nice.

Even if you're just commuting through, it's tempting to stop here and watch the traffic pass by.

The spot is half-open / half-covered and overall the ambience is really pleasant.

The lighting is favorably reddish and the music they had playing was charming, like 30s and 40s era classics.

The Food And Drink

City Lights does a basic but decent range of cocktails. We first asked for a Pimm's - which they'd run out of - then a Manhattan - which was also unavailable. We finally settled on a Bloody Mary (Rs. 800) which was well done. While we wouldn't recommend this place for the drinks selection, the execution isn't bad. 

Kaama wise, we tried the chili cheese bacon wrapped prawns (Rs. 1,200). While expensive, the combination was irresistible. It was actually quite good. Big prawns, wrapped in bacon, cheese and batter fried. Really good. Besides they have a range of bites from Rs. 400-700 and full range meals like nasi goreng and sandwiches.

Service

Service-wise, we found the place actually a bit lacking. We were seated by a nice young man, but he then disappeared. The bartenders paid attention to the foreign guests but basically ignored us. Even when we were the only people there they were gone or had their backs turned. Didn't really matter for a leisurely drink, but the service doesn't really seem oriented towards locals.

Conclusion

City Lights is a nice if bit pricey bar at the edge of the city. There's a nice view, great ambience and good food and drink. Service is a bit neglectful, but overall a nice place to have a drink.


TIP

The menu may say Pimm's, but nobody ever has it in stock.
