This space has been revamped as Ikoi Tei

Work In Progress is a Hilton run pub/cafe located in the Dutch Hospital Shopping Precinct. They've got an enviable front courtyard spot right next to the main entrance - ideal for whisking in wandering customers.

While the Dutch Hospital has quite distinctive establishments - Ministry of Crab for crab; the Heladiv Tea Club for tea and desserts, The Brewery by O! for booze - both Work In Progress and Harpo's Colombo Fort Cafe seem to stand as more casual eateries, not really defined by anything in particular but more just serving relatively affordable food in a fashionable location.

However, as we discovered, Work In Progress isn't just a random Lankan cafe serving up the burger/nasi goreng/pasta staples. The menu seems to veer quite strongly towards the Deutsch, offering the likes of schnitzel, bratwurst and brezel - the portmanteau used to denote a curious bread/pretzel union (although we wouldn't recommend it, it's slightly salty and strange).

The food at WIP is really quite good and the prices on the menu are affordable (but beware the post meal tax): Cajun modha served with rice (Rs. 900), chicken schnitzel served with potato salad and cranberry compote (Rs. 675), BBQ spare ribs with spicy wedges (Rs. 1225). There's little to complain about here. This is hearty, meaty food in a lively, pleasant location. Perhaps not great for vegetarians, but still good for everyone else.

A must try is the virgin cocktail, veline (Rs. 340). This consists of a genius, scrumptious duo of peach syrup and ginger ale. Probably not worth the price considering how simple a concoction it is, but it's really rather delicious - and until we've managed to locate some peach syrup for ourselves we'll definitely keep coming back for more.

The conclusion, WIP really isn't a bad place to grab some grub - especially if you want some strong stuff to line your stomach before a night out. The food is good, so is the location. You're unlikely to go wrong with an evening spent here.


TIP

Try the veline virgin cocktail, it's delicious.
